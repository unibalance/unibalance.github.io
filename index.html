800W Hub Motor

The new KS 14M comes with an stress tested top of the line hub motor capable of delivering a whopping 800W output power. Glide past peak hour traffic at upto 20 km/hr or pick it up and walk away with a breath of fresh air while everyone's waiting for the lights to turn green.

Built-in Bluetooth Connectivity

KS 14M connects seamlessly with your mobile device which lets you listen to music through built-in speakers, track your mileage, speed, trip parameters and other data including fellow Kingsong riders in your area.

Advanced Control Mechanism

The electric unicycle has one of the most beautiful control system designed in a vehicle yet. It becomes an extension of your body, almost responds to your thoughts and wishes.

Tested for Safety and Performance

Each KS 14M leaving Kingsong factory is tested against impeccable performance and safety standards. Rest assured it will never let you down.

Extremely Portable

KS 14M comes with a 174Wh battery pack sourced from the best Li-ion cell manufacturers that charges from 0-100% within 2 hours and gives an average-built rider a range of at least 12 kms on Indian roads. What's more, feeling like off-roading today? Yes you can!

Safety and Riding Pleasure

KS 14M comes with the best materials used for pedals, footboard grip, and protective foam padding which make riding a joy even on long tedious rides.
