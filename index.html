Powerful Hub Motor

The new KINGSONG electric unicycles come with a stress tested top of the line hub motor capable of delivering a whopping 800-1200W output power. Glide past peak hour traffic at upto 35 km/hr or pick it up and walk away with a breath of fresh air while everyone's waiting for the lights to turn green.

Built-in Bluetooth Connectivity

All KINGSONG electric unicycles connects seamlessly with your mobile device which lets you listen to through built-in speakers, track your mileage, speed, trip parameters and other data including fellow Kingsong riders in your area.

Advanced Control Mechanism

KINGSONG electric unicycles have one of the most beautiful control system designed in a vehicle yet. It becomes an extension of your body, almost responds to your thoughts and wishes.

Tested for Safety and Performance

KINGSONG electric unicycles comes with the best materials used for pedals, footboard grip, and protective foam padding which make riding a joy even on long tedious rides.

The hub-motor wheel brings you the most convenient form factor for personal mobility. Weighing at just about 12-17 KGs, KINGSONG electric unicycles can be carried around in all popular modes of personal transit while it carries you around the last mile, or even 70 km!

High Grade Li-Ion Battery Included

KINGSONG electric unicycles come with a Li-Ion battery pack sourced from the best Li-ion cell manufacturers that charges from 0-100% within 2-6 hours and gives an average-built rider a range of 12-70 kms on Indian roads. What's more, feeling like off-roading today? Yes you can!

FAQ

  • Can we rent it?

    You can join Bangalore unicycle club and ask the club members or can email us and we will try to connect you to riders in your area who are willing to lend their unicycle on rent

  • Do you always have new Stock?

    Yes, the unicycles are in high demand and we import regularly from China, assemble here in India

  • What about Battery, how long does it last?

    Our customers have riden more than 2000 and our pro riders more than 10000Km's, normally the li-ion battery has a shelf life of 3 years 1000 cycles after that the range decreases per charge but works great

  • How much a new battery would cost?

    INR 12000/- for KS 14M and INR 36000/- for KS 16S

  • Does this need pothole free road?

    No. You can ride it in any terrain. It all depends on the experience of the rider. The single tyre gives better maneuverability and control to the riders

  • Can it be taken on flights?

    Not yet. According to airline regulations, the battery should be less than 160Wh and the minimum battery capacity on our unicycles is 174Wh

  • Can we test ride it?

    If you are based out of Bangalore, we organise regular meetups in Cubbon Park, Bangalore. (Follow Bangalore Electric Unicycle Club on Facebook for more details)

  • Any video tutorial on how to learn to ride the future(we mean Electric Unicycles)?

    There are many videos available on youtube from different electric unicycle brand, please watch this video (link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p8T9q7VcQlM) to get started)
